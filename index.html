Adam Swift Lucas (b. 1983) is a graphic designer, publisher, and educator living and working in Cleveland, Ohio. He runs Specific Ideas a multidisciplinary creative practice that is one part graphic design studio, one part independent publishing house; and is an Associate Professor of Graphic Design at Cleveland Institute of Art (CIA). Adam also collaborates with Rachel Ferber on an exploratory, project-based design initiative called NEW NEW NEW.

Previously, Adam was an Assistant Professor of Graphic Design at Kansas City Art Institute; a Senior Designer at SYPartners; and a co-founder with Andrew LeClair of LeClair Lucas.
